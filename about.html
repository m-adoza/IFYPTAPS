About IntelliVerify

Empowering academic institutions with intelligent project topic management and plagiarism detection.

Our Mission

To simplify and secure the final year project approval process through intelligent automation, ensuring originality and academic integrity in higher education.

Our Vision

To become the leading academic project management platform across universities, powered by AI and designed for the future of education.

Core Values

Integrity, innovation, transparency, and excellence. We believe in using technology to uphold academic standards and empower students.

The Problem

Many universities still rely on manual, paper-based processes for project topic approval. This leads to duplicated topics, slow reviews, lost records, and inconsistent supervision.

The Solution

IntelliVerify digitizes the entire workflow. Students submit topics online, supervisors review them, administrators approve them, and an intelligent engine checks for plagiarism in real-time.

Built with Modern Tech

IntelliVerify is built on a robust, scalable, and cloud-native architecture ready for future AI expansion.
